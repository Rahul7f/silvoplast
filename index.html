Welcome to Silvo Plast

Silvo Plast is one of the leading manufacturers offering Full Service Plastic Manufacturing company engaged in providing High Class Plastic products using injection and blow molding technology. The products are consumed in various verticals including Pharmaceutical industry, Chemical industry, Consumer Goods industry comprising of Stationery, Art-n-Crafts & Cosmetics.

The company was started in the year 1993 in Mumbai, Maharashtra by Mr. Mandar Raut, having a vast experience in manufacturing of plastic injection and blow moulded bottles. The company later shifted its manufacturing base to Vasai Industrial Zone, close to NH8 highway to offer better packaging solutions for its wide clientele base.
